About il Tulipano

Il Tulipano began as one of the best Italian restaurants in New Jersey and celebrates the traditions of the Polimeni family’s Italian heritage. All our cuisine—from the fresh bread to the gelato—is prepared in-house, or fatto in casa, as they say in Italy. Our impeccable service and personal touch have made us one of the best wedding venues in NJ and highly-rated by our clients in New Jersey Bride, The Knot and Wedding Wire.


We bring world-class style and go beyond your expectations. Let us show you our trademark wedding and special event magic. Come visit our luxurious indoor and outdoor wedding spaces and see for yourself what all the buzz is about.
